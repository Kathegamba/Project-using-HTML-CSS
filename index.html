Withney International is a textile Colombian industry that it has been in the industry for about 25 years, designing and making clothes for lovers of knitters. Our techniques are based on the use of knitting machines with manual pattering. This manual pattering is achieved thanks to our staff who have been with us throughout time, bringing ideas, patterns, styles, and colors alive. Withney International vision has been recognized for our community as a textile industry number one providing them quality and comfortable knitters. Since we already achieved the goal, we are trespassing our borders to bring you our best, comfy, elegant, and stylish knitters, finally becoming Withney International.

Knitters’ designs are versatile and suitable for various occasions, whether it's at school, college, work, or simply relaxing at home. They are crafted to enhance your look for any event, be it parties, meetings, trips, birthdays, anniversaries, weddings, or festive seasons like Christmas. Their high quality and comfort ensure you won't want to step out without one. Perfect for all seasons—winter, spring, summer, or fall—they provide warmth while keeping your style fresh. So, no matter the weather or the occasion, knitted designs have you covered.

At our business, bringing our clients' visions to life is not just a goal but a passion. We thrive on collaborating with our customers, listening attentively to their ideas, and translating them into knitted designs that exude comfort and satisfaction. Whether it's a unique color palette, a special pattern, or a custom size, we're dedicated to personalizing each knitted piece to perfectly suit our clients' preferences and needs.
